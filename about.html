About Me

First-Generation College Student and Diversity Leadership Scholar at Babson College

My Bio

I am a first-generation student at Babson College and a recipient of the Diversity Leadership Scholarship, attending on a full ride. My journey began in 2016 when I joined SquashBusters, where I discovered squash, traveled throughout New England, and grew as both an athlete and a leader. In 2018, I earned first place at the SEA Individuals, and from 2021-2022, I served as team captain, leading my team through the full competitive season.

During high school, I expanded my leadership through Junior Achievement, where I created Hoger, a sweater company built around giving back to Lazarus House, a nonprofit supporting individuals experiencing homelessness. My involvement with JA also led to the honor of serving as the MC for its 75th Anniversary celebration.

At Babson, I continue to build on my commitment to community, entrepreneurship, and representation. As a first-gen student, I am driven to create opportunities for others and to use my experiences to uplift the communities that shaped me.
